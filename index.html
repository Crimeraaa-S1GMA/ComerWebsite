Inspired by titles like Super Mario Bros and many other platformer games, Comer takes you through a journey to discover the secrets of the sewers which you had accidentally ended up in after returning home from a long day of work.

Along the way, you will also find many dangers that await you, such as nasty enemies, deep black pits, even more nasty spikes, and much more.

This game was made almost entirely by 1 person, crimeraaa, except for the soundtrack by Quabanos.

FAQ

(Not really) Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When is this game released?
A: Comer is intended to be released before the 1st of July.

Q: How many levels are there?
A: The game has 20 planned main levels, alongside that a few secret ones and a bonus one.

Q: How much will it cost?
A: Comer will be available to download for completely free. This generous price will stay the same forever.

Q: Why is this game's story taking place in the sewers?
A: I had the very stupid idea of doing that when I was first planning out this game, and decided to go with the theme for the sake of it. I understand that not everyone might particularly like that this happened, but there are other platformer games you can play if you hate this idea that much.

Q: What platforms will Comer support?
A: Comer currently supports all mainstream desktop platforms (Windows, Mac, Linux), Android and also has a HTML5 version.
